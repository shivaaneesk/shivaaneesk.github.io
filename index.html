A Systematic Evaluation of Vision-Language Models for News Image Retrieval: A Two-Stage Retrieve-and-Rerank Pipeline Analysis

MediaEval 2025 Workshop (CEUR-WS)

To improve automated news image retrieval, we developed a "Retrieve-and-Rerank" pipeline using OpenCLIP ViT-bigG-14. This study compares our method against four other state-of-the-art approaches.
